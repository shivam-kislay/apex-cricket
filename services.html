Apex Cricket Academy

The Academy is run in Cork and caters for U13 to U19 age groups. Douglas Community School offers Munster’s only indoor net facility where the players develop their skills in an individual and group environment. Individual player profiles and training diaries are used to help assess and measure their progress and bespoke measurable programs are created to ensure that each player’s required outcomes are achieved. Video analysis is used regularly to enhance each players learning but the key to long-term sustained goals being achieved is the player is always involved in their development. Apex Academy players will be part of an annual trip to an English County set up, where they will enjoy the chance to train at professional indoor centres and get the chance to work with County Head Coaches

Adult Group Workshops

Apex Cricket offers group coaching where small or large numbers can be catered for. These sessions are mainly work shop based where specific goals are nominated by players who want to work on different skill sets that are appropriate and relevant to them. They can be delivered in their clubs or at a venue where the required facilities are available. This is geared towards more towards adult players who represent their club at various levels. Its designed for players who harbour ambitions of playing at a higher level within their clubs or at a representative level.

One on One Coaching

Apex Cricket offers one on one coaching for any age group and this is an area where the player can really focus on key areas that will make a big difference to their all-round game. It’s a chance to identify specific performance factors, mental, physical, tactical, technical and lifestyle that can be prioritised so as to have the biggest immediate impact. Strengths become super-strengths and developmental areas become manageable. These sessions can be delivered at a venue that suits the players

Coach Education

Apex Cricket can collaborate with club and provincial coached to identify the specific needs withtin their enviroments, enabling them to progress and develop in order to deliver the best possible programmes With experience in coaching at all environments from grass roots level in schools to Senior Mens National Side, as well as having a level 3 tutoring accreditation, all coaches will benefit from learning more about their style and the need to constantly evolve their “what to” and “how to” coach skills.

Summer Camp

Apex Cricket offers a facility where Summer Camps can be run within clubs on dates that suit during the season. A coaching team, which can include the respective clubs own coaches, will run week long or multi day camps in a safe and fun environment. All equipment will be supplied and the camp can include specific topics that are relevant to each club’s needs. The option of a multi-sport camp is available, where sports, where there is cross-over that benefits cricket, can also be delivered.

Club Program Reviews

Apex Cricket can offer a review of current practices within clubs with regard to kids and adult coaching program’s. There is a need constantly update the content of clubs program’s when it comes to the appropriate skills being delivered along the Player Development Pathway. Research has shown that delivering the age and skill appropriate topics are key to a player long term development. Peak Height Velocity (growth spurts) can warrant slight variations in program delivery, therefore the need to monitor programs has become more important than ever
