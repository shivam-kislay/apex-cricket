Ted Williamson

Role: Head Coach

Batting Style: Left-Hand bat

Bio

As a player, Ted represented Munster at all levels from U13 to the Mens Senior side. He was capped for Ireland at U21, U23, Ireland A and Senior Level . He has been involved in coaching in Ireland for over 20 years at every level from grass roots to the Senior National men's side He holds a UKCC ECB Level 4 Master Coach in Elite Cricket Coaching with a Post Graduate Diploma in Personal & Professional Development from the University of Gloucester . He also holds an MSc (Masters) in Elite Cricket Coaching. He is also an ECB Qualified Tutor and Assessor, capable of delivering on UKCC Level 3 Performance Coach Courses He was worked in Ireland U17 and U19 environments and has recently been the Head Coach of the Leinster Lightning which won the treble two years running. He is the current Head Coach of the Munster Reds and Munster Heat and is the MCU Pathway Manager His Coaching Philosophy is one of player autonomy where the players will have ownership of their own games. The development of Independent Thinking Cricketers is a corner stone of his coaching process and he supports the players in developing an in-depth understanding of their own games and the game itself. The aim is to see players evolve through embracing an work ethic of self-challenge and continuous improvement
