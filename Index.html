About Us

At Apex Cricket, we offer a wide range of services to individuals and groups, helping people of all ages and skill levels maximise their playing ability. Our coaching philosophy involves the athlete in their own learning and focuses on developing the person as well as the player. To achieve this, and in line with the most up-to-date research, we believe that taking a holistic approach is the best way to ensure both short- and long-term goals are met.

What We Offer

The Apex Cricket Academy

The Academy is run in Cork and caters for U13 to U19 age groups.

Adult Group Workshops

Apex Cricket offers group coaching where small or large numbers can be catered for.

One on One Coaching

Apex Cricket offers one on one coaching for any age group and this is an area where the player can really focus on key areas that will make a big difference to their all-round game.

Coach Education

Apex Cricket can collaborate with club and provincial coached to identify the specific needs withtin their enviroments, enabling them to progress and develop in order to deliver the best possible programmes

Summer Camps

Apex Cricket offers a facility where Summer Camps can be run within clubs on dates that suit during the season.

Club Program Reviews

Apex Cricket can offer a review of current practices within clubs with regard to kids and adult coaching program’s.
